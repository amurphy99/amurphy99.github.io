I presented a live demo of our robotic interface for an end-to-end conversational speech system designed to support natural, multi-turn interaction for older adults. The demo showcased real-time ASR/TTS + LLM conversation and how the platform can be extended to surface speech-based analytics for research in cognitive health monitoring.
